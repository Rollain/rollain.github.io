I am a portrait painter from East London based in Brighton.
I work primarily in oils. I completed my foundation year at the royal
drawing school in london and went on to study Fine Art painting
at the university of brighton. My work is focused on figurative portraiture,
and exploring it through my own lense in my own way.

I am open to commissions and the sale of original pieces
all prices are negotiated on a case by case basis.
